Professional Development Model:

This model helps the Observers improve their Observation skills. An Assessor will only see a Scoring Accuracy Status Report while the Observer will also receive Formative Feedback.

Oversight Model:

This model provides Assessors with Performance Level details (based on cut scores) and allows them to give feedback to Observers. The report that Observers receive do not include Performance Level details, they see only Scoring Accuracy and Formative Feedback.

Accountability Model:

Scoring Accuracy Status Report, Formative Feedback and Performance Level details are provided to both Assessors and Observers.
